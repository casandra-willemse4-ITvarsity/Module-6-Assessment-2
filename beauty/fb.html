Fragrance Boutique

Our humble beginnings began with the
dream of creating a luxury boutique-style
environment where fragrance
lovers could discover the
joy of reverse engineered fragrances,
generic fragrances packaged in no nonsense
bottles,
without the international marketing & advertising costs.
We believe that “you wear the fragrance, not the packaging” & our
first slogan was “the price brings you in & the
quality brings you back”,
this still stands today.
