Cashbuild

Cashbuild is the largest retailer of building materials and associated products,
selling directly to cash-paying customers through its
319 (and growing) stores in South Africa, Namibia,
Lesotho, Botswana, Swaziland, Malawi and Zambia.
Cashbuild which employs 6 238 people, comprises
Cashbuild Limited and the operating subsidiaries registered in
the abovementioned countries. Cashbuild is the
first choice retailer in its chosen field in all the regions
in which it operates. It achieves this by carrying
a focused in-depth quality product range at the most competitive
prices, to meet the needs of the local market for homebuilders,
home improvers, contractors, farmers, traders and any
persons wanting to purchase our quality
building materials for cash. Cashbuild shares have
been listed on the JSE Securities Exchange since 1986.
