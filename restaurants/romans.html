Romans

It all started in 1993 when owner Mr. A. Nicolakakis,
a restauranteur by trade, purchased a flagging
pizza restaurant with the intention
of turning it around with his extensive
knowledge and expertise. Instead of
changing prices he
decided to give two pizzas for the price of one.
That is when Little Caesar’s came into
being, eventually leading
to the “birth” of Roman’s Pizza in 2002.
Today, Roman’s Pizza has some of the busiest
pizzerias in the country with some
of our stores selling up to and even at times,
in excess of, 2,000 pizzas per night.
